Description:

Cookies the lazy way. Might not taste good as home-made, but a whole lot easier.

Ingredients

  • I can't really think of anything else

Steps

  1. HEAT oven to 350ÂºF(449.8K)
  2. SPOON and SHAPE rounded teaspoonfuls of dough into 1-inch balls. PLACE 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet. FLATTEN in crisscross pattern, using fork dipped in sugar.
  3. BAKE 9 to 14 minutes or until golden brown. COOL 1 minute; remove from cookie sheet.
